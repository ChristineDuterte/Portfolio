SMEasy Business Recommender

From Thesis: DATA ANALYTICS-BASED SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISE BUSINESS POTENTIAL ADVISOR

SMEasy is a web application designed to provide smart business recommendations using data analytics. By analyzing local market trends, population data, and existing business distributions, the system helps users identify the most promising small and medium enterprise (SME) opportunities within Cabuyao. Whether users are looking to start a business or expand an existing one, the platform offers insights to support data-driven decisions and promote strategic business planning.

Tools: HTML CSS JavaScript PostgreSQL Node.js

Screenshots

Mapping

Shows a map of Cabuyao with blue pins marking barangays. Users can search for registered small and medium enterprises, view their locations, and see business info. Floating buttons let users filter results by category.

Data Visualization

Displays detailed barangay and category data through graphs and tables. Users can filter data to analyze businesses, population, market demand, and gaps.

Analysis

Users can find the top 3 barangays for a business category or the top 3 business categories for a barangay. Each recommendation includes a score and detailed info to guide decisions.

Admin Page

Central management area for admins to add, edit, or delete business and survey data. Keeps the database updated and accurate, ensuring data integrity across the platform.
